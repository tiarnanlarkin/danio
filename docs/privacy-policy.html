🐟 Danio Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

The short version: We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

None. Danio operates entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

All data you enter into the app — including tank information, water parameters, livestock records, learning progress, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only.

2. Data Storage

  • Local storage only: All app data is stored in your device's local database (SQLite)
  • No cloud sync: Your data is not uploaded to any cloud service
  • No accounts required: You don't need to create an account to use Danio
  • No analytics: We don't use any analytics or tracking tools

3. Third-Party Services

Danio does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

If you choose to use the optional AI-powered features (Fish ID, Symptom Triage), image data is processed via OpenAI's API according to their privacy policy. These features are entirely optional and clearly labelled.

4. Children's Privacy

Danio does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the app doesn't collect any personal data from any user, it is safe for users of all ages.

5. Permissions

Danio may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera (optional): Only used for Fish & Plant ID feature. Photos are processed and not stored externally.
  • Notifications (optional): Used for maintenance reminders you set up.
  • Storage (optional): Used for backup/restore of your local data.

All permissions are optional and the app works without them.

6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear Data" option in the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

7. Changes to This Policy

If we add features that involve data collection in future versions, we will update this policy and notify you within the app. We will always prioritise your privacy.
