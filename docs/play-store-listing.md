## Full Description (4,000 chars max)

🐟 Danio — Duolingo for Fishkeeping

The first app that makes learning aquarium keeping fun, structured, and rewarding. Whether you're setting up your first tank or levelling up your planted paradise, Danio guides you every step of the way.

📚 LEARN LIKE A PRO
• 12 structured lessons covering everything from tank cycling to advanced water chemistry
• 11 practice quizzes with spaced repetition to lock in knowledge
• 20+ expert tips you can apply right away
• Progress tracking with XP, levels, and daily streaks

🏆 STAY MOTIVATED
• Earn 20+ achievements as you grow your skills
• Daily goals keep you on track
• Level up your fishkeeper rank from Beginner to Master
• Beautiful celebrations when you hit milestones

🐠 MANAGE YOUR TANKS
• Track unlimited aquariums with individual settings
• Log water parameters (pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, temperature)
• Visualise trends over time with beautiful charts
• Smart maintenance reminders so you never miss a water change

🔬 SPECIES DATABASE
• 45+ freshwater species with detailed care requirements
• Compatibility checker to plan your community tank
• Beautiful species profiles with key stats

🏠 YOUR COSY AQUARIUM ROOM
• Personalise your virtual fishkeeper's room
• Unlock themes, decorations, and room upgrades
• Watch your room evolve as you progress

🎨 BEAUTIFUL DESIGN
• Warm, inviting golden-hour aesthetic
• Painterly illustrations inspired by Pixar and DreamWorks
• Smooth animations and satisfying micro-interactions
• Dark mode support

🔒 PRIVACY-FIRST
• All data stored locally on your device
• No account required
• No ads
• Your hobby, your data

Perfect for:
✅ Complete beginners starting their first tank
✅ Intermediate hobbyists wanting to deepen their knowledge
✅ Experienced keepers who want a beautiful tracking tool
✅ Anyone who loves fish and wants to learn more!

Download Danio and start your fishkeeping journey today. 🐟
