# Celebration Audio Assets

This directory contains sound effects for celebration animations in the Aquarium App.

## Required Audio Files

### 1. **fanfare.mp3** (Lesson Completion)
- **Duration:** 2-3 seconds
- **Style:** Bright, celebratory trumpet fanfare
- **Use:** Lesson completion, perfect score
- **Inspiration:** Duolingo lesson complete sound
- **Suggested source:** [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/fanfare/) or [Freesound](https://freesound.org/)
- **Keywords:** "success fanfare", "achievement sound", "victory trumpet"

### 2. **chime.mp3** (Achievement Unlock)
- **Duration:** 1-2 seconds
- **Style:** Magical chime or bell sound
- **Use:** Achievement badge unlock, XP milestone
- **Inspiration:** iOS notification chime, achievement unlock
- **Suggested source:** [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/chime/) or [Freesound](https://freesound.org/)
- **Keywords:** "magic chime", "achievement bell", "notification sound"

### 3. **applause.mp3** (Streak Milestone)
- **Duration:** 2-4 seconds
- **Style:** Light applause or crowd cheer
- **Use:** 7-day streak, major milestones
- **Inspiration:** Duolingo streak celebration
- **Suggested source:** [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/applause/) or [Freesound](https://freesound.org/)
- **Keywords:** "applause", "crowd cheer", "celebration"

### 4. **fireworks.mp3** (Epic Celebration)
- **Duration:** 3-5 seconds
- **Style:** Fireworks explosion with sparkle
- **Use:** Level up, platinum achievements, 100+ day streaks
- **Inspiration:** New Year's Eve fireworks
- **Suggested source:** [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/fireworks/) or [Freesound](https://freesound.org/)
- **Keywords:** "fireworks", "celebration explosion", "sparkle"

### 5. **whoosh.mp3** (Quick Win)
- **Duration:** 0.5-1 second
- **Style:** Quick uplifting whoosh
- **Use:** Small XP gains, quick victories
- **Inspiration:** iOS swipe sound
- **Suggested source:** [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/whoosh/) or [Freesound](https://freesound.org/)
- **Keywords:** "whoosh", "swipe", "quick success"

## Audio Specifications

- **Format:** MP3 (for cross-platform compatibility)
- **Sample Rate:** 44.1 kHz or 48 kHz
- **Bit Rate:** 128-192 kbps
- **Channels:** Stereo
- **Normalization:** -3dB to -1dB peak to prevent clipping

## Testing

After adding audio files:
1. Test on both Android and iOS
2. Verify volume levels are consistent
3. Check that sounds respect user's reduced motion settings
4. Test with device on silent/vibrate mode
5. Ensure sounds don't overlap awkwardly

## Placeholder Files

Until proper audio files are added, the celebration service will work silently.
The code is designed to fail gracefully if audio files are missing.
