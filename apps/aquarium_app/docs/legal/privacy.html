1. Introduction

Danio ("the App") is developed and operated by Tiarnan Larkin ("we", "us", "our"), an independent developer based in the United Kingdom. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Danio is designed to work 100% offline by default. You are never required to create an account or enable cloud features. All core functionality works entirely on your device with no data leaving it unless you explicitly opt in to features that require it.

3. Information We Collect

📱 Local App Data (stored on your device only)

Tank information, livestock records, water test logs, maintenance logs, photos you add to your journal, learning progress (lessons, quiz scores, XP, streaks, achievements), app settings, and scheduled reminders. All stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and JSON files. This data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

📊 Firebase Analytics (consent-based, opt-in)

When you give your consent on first launch, we collect anonymous usage data including screen views and navigation events via Firebase Analytics. This helps us understand how the app is used and improve it. Data is shared with Google LLC (USA). You can withdraw consent at any time in Settings → Privacy.

🐛 Firebase Crashlytics (consent-based)

When you consent, crash reports are collected automatically when the app encounters an error. These contain device OS version, app version, and error stack traces. No personal data is included in crash logs. Crash reports are retained for 90 days, then deleted. Data is processed by Google LLC (USA). Crashlytics is disabled if you decline consent.

🤖 OpenAI API — AI-Powered Features (optional, consent-based)

When you use AI-powered features, the following data may be sent to OpenAI Inc. (USA). You must accept a one-time disclosure before any data is sent to OpenAI:

  • Fish ID: Photos you submit are sent to OpenAI for species identification.
  • Symptom Triage: Symptom descriptions and water parameter values you enter are sent to OpenAI for fish health diagnosis.
  • Weekly Planner: Your tank names, setup details (volume, type), and livestock information (species, counts) are sent to OpenAI to generate a personalised maintenance schedule.
  • Anomaly Detector: Water parameter data (pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, temperature readings) is sent to OpenAI for anomaly explanation and remediation advice.

All data sent to OpenAI is retained for a maximum of 30 days, then automatically deleted. OpenAI does not use API data to train their models. See OpenAI's API Data Usage Policy.

☁️ Supabase — Cloud Sync (optional, currently dormant)

The app includes code for optional cloud sync via Supabase. This feature is not currently active. When activated in a future update, it will store your email and sync data in Supabase (EU region), encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3. We will update this policy and request explicit consent before activation.

7. Data Retention

  • Local app data: Stored on your device indefinitely until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Firebase Analytics data: 26 months (Google's standard retention period)
  • Firebase Crashlytics crash logs: 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • OpenAI AI feature data: Maximum 30 days on OpenAI's servers, then automatically deleted
  • Cloud sync data (Supabase): Retained while your account is active; permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

8. Data Storage & Security

  • All network transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Supabase connections use TLS 1.3
  • Photo backups (when cloud sync active) are encrypted with AES-256 before upload
  • Local data is protected by your device's own security (lock screen, device encryption)
  • No passwords are stored by us — authentication uses secure token-based flows
  • We do not collect payment information — handled by Google Play/Apple App Store

9. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON export available in the app)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

How to Exercise Your Rights

  • In-app (delete): Settings → Delete My Data
  • In-app (export): Settings → Backup & Restore → Export
  • In-app (consent): Settings → Privacy to manage analytics consent
  • By email: larkintiarnanbizz@gmail.com — we will respond within 30 days

10. Opting Out of Analytics & Crashlytics

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Navigate to Privacy
  3. Toggle the combined Analytics & Crash Reports setting off

When analytics is disabled, no usage events are sent to Google. When crash reporting is disabled, no crash data is sent to Firebase Crashlytics.
