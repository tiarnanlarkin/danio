Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aquarium App ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address (if you choose to create an account)
  • Tank Data: Information about your aquariums, fish, plants, water parameters, and maintenance logs
  • Photos: Images you upload to document your aquarium journey
  • Learning Progress: Lesson completion, quiz scores, XP, and achievements

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, interaction patterns
  • Analytics: Performance metrics to improve app stability

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise location data
  • We do not access your contacts or calendar
  • We do not collect payment information (handled securely by app stores)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: Store your tank data, track progress, deliver personalized content
  • Improve App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Notifications: Send reminders for water changes, feeding, and lesson streaks (with your permission)
  • Community Features: Enable optional social features like leaderboards and friends (opt-in only)

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync
  • We cannot access your data
  • You have complete control and privacy

4.2 Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud sync:

  • Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Data is stored on secure servers (Firebase/Google Cloud)
  • You can disable sync anytime and delete your cloud data

4.3 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard encryption
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • No sale of user data to third parties

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Cloud sync and authentication (optional)
  • Analytics Services: Crash reporting and performance monitoring (anonymized)
  • App Stores: Google Play / Apple App Store for distribution and payments

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Access & Control

  • View Your Data: All data visible in-app settings
  • Export Data: Download your data as CSV/JSON
  • Delete Data: Delete specific entries or entire account
  • Disable Sync: Turn off cloud sync anytime

6.2 Opt-Out Options

  • Analytics: Disable analytics in app settings
  • Notifications: Manage notification preferences anytime
  • Social Features: All community features are opt-in

6.3 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete it
  • Cloud Data: Retained while your account is active or for 90 days after deletion request
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data retained for up to 2 years for improvement purposes

9. International Data Transfers

If you use cloud sync, your data may be transferred to and stored on servers in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data according to this privacy policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if you provided one)
  • Update to "Last Updated" date at the top

Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@aquariumapp.com
  • In-App: Settings → Help & Support → Contact

We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.

12. Legal Basis (GDPR)

For EU users, our legal basis for processing your data:

  • Contract Performance: To provide app services you requested
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve app functionality and security
  • Consent: For optional features like cloud sync, analytics, notifications
