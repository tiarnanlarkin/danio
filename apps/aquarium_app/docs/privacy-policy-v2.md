## 1. What Data We Collect and Why

### 1.1 Core App Data (stored locally on your device)

| Data | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Tank configurations, fish logs, water parameters, achievements, streaks | Core app functionality | Contract — Art 6(1)(b) GDPR |
| User preferences (theme, units, notifications) | App personalisation | Contract — Art 6(1)(b) GDPR |

This data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to any server unless you explicitly use the Export feature.

### 1.2 Analytics & Crash Reporting (requires your consent)

| Data | Third Party | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|------|-------------|---------|-------------|
| App interaction events (6 events: lesson completion, tank creation, fish added, streak milestones, feature usage, fish ID usage) | Google (Firebase Analytics) | Understand how features are used to improve the app | Consent — Art 6(1)(a) GDPR |
| Android Advertising ID (AAID) | Google (Firebase Analytics) | Aggregate usage analytics | Consent — Art 6(1)(a) GDPR |
| Crash logs, stack traces, device info | Google (Firebase Crashlytics) | Diagnose and fix bugs | Legitimate interest — Art 6(1)(f) GDPR |

**Analytics and crash reporting are disabled by default.** You are asked for consent during onboarding. You can change your choice at any time in Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Crash Reports.

### 1.3 AI Fish Identification (optional feature)

| Data | Third Party | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|------|-------------|---------|-------------|
| Photos of fish (camera or gallery) | OpenAI (US servers) | AI-powered fish species identification | Consent — Art 6(1)(a) GDPR |

When you use the **Fish ID** feature, your photo is sent to OpenAI's API servers in the United States for analysis. **Photos are transmitted via HTTPS and may be retained by OpenAI for up to 30 days** in accordance with their data retention policy. Danio does not permanently store your photos on any server.

You are never required to use Fish ID — the app functions fully without it.

## 4. Your Rights Under GDPR / UK GDPR

You have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data (Art 15)
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data (Art 16)
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten") (Art 17)
- **Restrict processing** (Art 18)
- **Data portability** (Art 20) — use Settings → Data → Export All Data
- **Object to processing** (Art 21)
- **Withdraw consent** at any time without affecting prior lawful processing (Art 7(3))

To exercise any of these rights, email **privacy@tiarnanlarkin.com**.

---

## 5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data in two ways:

1. **In-app:** Go to **Settings → Danger Zone → Delete My Data**. This permanently erases all local data (tanks, progress, achievements, preferences) and resets the app to its initial state. This action cannot be undone.

2. **Email:** Send a deletion request to **privacy@tiarnanlarkin.com** for any server-side data (analytics, crash logs). We will process your request within 30 days.

Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.
