# Danio — Screenshot Strategy

> Last updated: 28 February 2026

---

## Design Principles

1. **First 3 screenshots are make-or-break** — 70%+ of users never scroll past them
2. **Benefit-led captions** — sell the outcome, not the feature
3. **Consistent brand palette** — Danio aquatic blues/teals/greens with white text overlays
4. **Show real UI** — actual app screens inside device frames, not mockups
5. **One clear message per screenshot** — don't cram multiple features into one image

## 8-Screenshot Sequence

### Screenshot 1 — THE HOOK
**Caption:** `Learn fishkeeping step by step 🐠`
**Sub-caption:** `Bite-sized lessons for every level`

**What to show:**
- Lesson module screen — "The Nitrogen Cycle" lesson card open
- Colourful module icons visible in background (water chemistry, species care, tank setup)
- Progress bar showing 30% through beginner path
- XP counter and streak flame in the header
- Bright, inviting — this is the first thing people see

**Why first:** Education is Danio's unique differentiator. No competitor has this. Lead with it.

---

### Screenshot 2 — CORE VALUE
**Caption:** `Track your tank like a pro`
**Sub-caption:** `Water parameters at a glance`

**What to show:**
- Water parameter dashboard with clean trend graph (pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate over 30 days)
- Green "healthy" indicators on current readings
- Last test date and next reminder timestamp
- A clean, modern dashboard — NOT a cluttered spreadsheet look

**Why second:** Tank tracking is the daily-use feature that creates habit. Show it's beautiful AND useful.

---

### Screenshot 3 — DISCOVERY
**Caption:** `500+ species at your fingertips`
**Sub-caption:** `Find the perfect fish for your tank`

**What to show:**
- Species browser grid view — beautiful fish photos in card layout
- Mix of popular species visible: Betta, Neon Tetra, Corydoras, Cherry Shrimp, Angelfish
- Difficulty rating badges on each card (Easy 🟢, Medium 🟡, Advanced 🔴)
- Search bar at top
- One card partially expanded to show care quick-reference

**Why third:** Species browsing is the "fun" feature that hooks browsing users. Visual and colourful.

---

### Screenshot 4 — HABIT FORMATION
**Caption:** `Never miss a water change 🔔`
**Sub-caption:** `Smart reminders keep your fish happy`

**What to show:**
- Maintenance schedule screen with upcoming tasks
- "Water Change — Due Today" highlighted with a gentle alert
- Streak counter showing "12 day streak 🔥"
- Completed tasks checked off (filter clean ✅, feed ✅)
- Weekly calendar view with colour-coded maintenance dots

**Why fourth:** Retention mechanic. Shows the app becomes part of your daily routine.

---

### Screenshot 5 — GAMIFICATION
**Caption:** `Level up your fishkeeping 🏆`
**Sub-caption:** `Earn badges, build streaks, become a Master Aquarist`

**What to show:**
- Achievement/profile screen showing:
  - Current level: "Intermediate Aquarist" with progress bar to next level
  - Recently earned badges: "First Cycle Complete 🔄", "7-Day Streak 🔥", "Species Expert 🐟"
  - XP counter and total lessons completed
  - Locked badges visible (greyed out) to create aspiration
- Fun, colourful badge designs — NOT generic trophy icons

**Why fifth:** This is the "sticky" factor. Shows there's always something to work towards.

---

### Screenshot 6 — PROBLEM SOLVER
**Caption:** `Will these fish get along? 🤔`
**Sub-caption:** `Compatibility checker stops costly mistakes`

**What to show:**
- Compatibility checker screen with a tank stocking plan
- Two fish being compared with green "Compatible ✅" result
- Tank capacity meter (e.g., "75% stocked — room for 2 more small fish")
- Warning flag on an incompatible pairing (e.g., "⚠️ Betta + Gourami — territorial conflict")
- Clear, actionable UI that prevents beginner mistakes

**Why sixth:** Solves a real pain point that costs hobbyists money and fish lives. High emotional value.

---

### Screenshot 7 — VISUAL PROGRESS
**Caption:** `Watch your tank evolve 📸`
**Sub-caption:** `Track your aquascape journey over time`

**What to show:**
- Photo timeline view showing the same tank at 3 time points:
  - Day 1: Fresh setup, bare substrate, new plants
  - Month 1: Plants growing in, slight algae
  - Month 3: Lush, full planted tank — transformation complete
- Date stamps on each photo
- Optional: before/after slider or side-by-side comparison

**Why seventh:** Highly shareable, aspirational content. Makes people want to document their own tanks.

---

### Screenshot 8 — SOCIAL PROOF
**Caption:** `Join thousands of fishkeepers 🌊`
**Sub-caption:** `Free to start. Built for the hobby.`

**What to show:**
- Collage/mosaic layout combining:
  - App rating (4.8 ★) with review count
  - Quick testimonial quotes from users
  - Mix of app UI previews (lessons, tracking, species)
  - Download counter or user count (update as milestones hit)
  - "Free on Google Play" badge
- This is the closing CTA screenshot — summarise the value proposition

**Why last:** Reinforces trust and gives a final push to install. Social proof closes the deal.

## Screenshot Text Style Guide

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| **Caption font** | Bold sans-serif (Inter Bold, SF Pro Bold, or Google Sans Bold) |
| **Caption size** | 48–64pt (readable at thumbnail size) |
| **Sub-caption** | Regular weight, 32–40pt, slightly lower opacity (80%) |
| **Text colour** | White on dark overlay, OR dark text on light card |
| **Background** | Gradient overlay on device frame — aquatic blue (#0EA5E9) to teal (#14B8A6) |
| **Device frame** | Thin, modern — Pixel 8 or frameless with subtle shadow |
| **Padding** | Generous — don't crowd the device frame |
